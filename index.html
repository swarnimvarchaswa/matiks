The House System üè°

Ok so here's what I'm thinking...

When users first login, they choose from 4 houses/colors (red, blue, green, yellow).

But the cool part? The app starts mostly in black & white.

As they climb the ranks and hit certain milestones (like top 10% in their house), they gradually unlock their house's colored UI.

Suddenly their achievement is visible every time they open the app!

It creates this instant community vibe where users feel connected to others in their house, plus that healthy competition between houses drives engagement like crazy.

Here's the thing ‚Äì nobody wants generic math problems. Students want stuff that's relevant to THEIR goals.
